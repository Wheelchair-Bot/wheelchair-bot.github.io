About the Project

Wheelchair Bot is an innovative project aimed at developing an autonomous navigation system for wheelchairs. Our mission is to enhance the independence and mobility of wheelchair users through cutting-edge robotics and AI technology.

By integrating advanced sensors, machine learning algorithms, and intuitive controls, we're creating a system that can help users navigate complex environments safely and efficiently.
